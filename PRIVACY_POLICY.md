# Privacy Policy — Threads Spoiler Revealer

**Last updated:** March 19, 2026

## Overview

Threads Spoiler Revealer is a browser extension that automatically reveals hidden spoiler content on Threads (threads.net). Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

This extension **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**. It does not track your browsing activity, and it does not communicate with any external servers.

## Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`) solely to save your preferences:

- Whether auto-reveal is enabled or disabled
- Whether spoiler highlighting is enabled or disabled

These preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

## Permissions

- **Host permissions** (`threads.net`, `threads.com`): Required to detect and reveal spoiler-tagged content on Threads pages. The extension only runs on Threads domains.
- **Storage permission**: Required to save your toggle preferences locally.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in this document with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
